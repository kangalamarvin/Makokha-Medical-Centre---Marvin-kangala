Flexible HPI approach:
  • Use SOCRATES/OPQRST mainly for pain complaints.
  • For non-pain complaints (vomiting, diarrhea, cough, fever, etc.), document symptom-specific chronology and pattern.
  • Always capture associated symptoms, risk exposures, prior treatment, and danger signs.
  • Describe impact on feeding, hydration, sleep, work, or daily activities.
